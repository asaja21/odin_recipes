Kebab Recipe

Plate of Kebab

Juicy, spiced meat slowly roasted to perfection, wrapped in a warm flatbread with crisp fresh vegetables and a generous drizzle of creamy garlic sauce. A street food classic that delivers bold flavors in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 4 flatbreads (pita or lavash)
  • 600 grams lamb or beef, thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 0.5 teaspoon turmeric
  • 0.5 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon black pepper
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, sliced
  • 1 cucumber, sliced
  • 100 grams iceberg lettuce, shredded
  • 150 grams plain yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice

Steps

  1. Marinate the meat: In a bowl, combine 600 grams sliced meat with 2 tablespoons olive oil, 3 garlic cloves minced, 1 teaspoon cumin, 1 teaspoon paprika, 0.5 teaspoon turmeric, 0.5 teaspoon cinnamon, 1 teaspoon salt, and 0.5 teaspoon black pepper. Mix well and let marinate for at least 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the meat: Heat a skillet or grill pan over high heat. Cook the marinated meat with 1 onion thinly sliced for 8 to 10 minutes, stirring regularly, until browned and cooked through.
  3. Make the garlic sauce: Mix 150 grams plain yogurt with 1 tablespoon lemon juice and a pinch of salt. Stir well until smooth.
  4. Warm the flatbreads: Heat 4 flatbreads in a dry pan for 1 to 2 minutes on each side until soft and warm.
  5. Assemble and serve: Lay each flatbread flat. Add a layer of 100 grams shredded lettuce, sliced tomatoes, sliced cucumber, then top with the cooked meat. Drizzle generously with garlic sauce, wrap tightly and serve immediately.
