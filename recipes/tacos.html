Tacos Recipe

Plate of Tacos

Soft corn tortillas loaded with smoky seasoned beef, topped with fresh salsa, creamy guacamole, and a squeeze of lime. A vibrant Mexican classic bursting with color, texture, and flavor.

Ingredients

  • 8 small corn or flour tortillas
  • 500 grams ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 0.5 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 0.5 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 0.5 red onion, finely diced
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 2 avocados, mashed
  • 100 grams shredded cheddar cheese
  • 100 grams sour cream
  • 1 handful fresh cilantro, chopped

Steps

  1. Cook the beef: Heat 1 tablespoon olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté 1 onion finely chopped for 3 minutes, then add 2 garlic cloves minced. Add 500 grams ground beef and cook for 6 to 8 minutes until browned. Season with 1 teaspoon cumin, 1 teaspoon chili powder, 0.5 teaspoon smoked paprika, 1 teaspoon salt, and 0.5 teaspoon black pepper. Stir well and simmer for 5 minutes.
  2. Make the salsa: In a bowl, combine 2 tomatoes diced, 0.5 red onion finely diced, half the lime juice, and a pinch of salt. Mix well and set aside.
  3. Make the guacamole: Mash 2 avocados with the remaining lime juice and a pinch of salt until smooth but slightly chunky.
  4. Warm the tortillas: Heat 8 tortillas in a dry pan for 1 minute on each side, or wrap them in a damp paper towel and microwave for 30 seconds.
  5. Assemble and serve: Fill each tortilla with the seasoned beef, a spoonful of salsa, a dollop of guacamole, 100 grams shredded cheddar, and 100 grams sour cream. Finish with fresh cilantro and serve immediately.
