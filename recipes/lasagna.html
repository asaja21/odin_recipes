Lasagna Recipe

Plate of Lasagna

Melt-in-your-mouth lasagna, generously layered with slow-cooked meat and creamy béchamel, perfectly golden and bubbling. A timeless Italian classic that never fails to comfort.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna sheets
  • 500 grams ground beef
  • 400 grams crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 500 milliliters whole milk
  • 40 grams butter
  • 40 grams all-purpose flour
  • 80 grams grated parmesan
  • 150 grams grated mozzarella
  • 1 teaspoons salt
  • 0.5 teaspoons black pepper
  • 0.3 teaspoons nutmeg

Steps

  1. Cook the meat sauce: Heat 2 tablespoons olive oil in a pan over medium heat. Sauté 1 onion, finely chopped for 20 minutes , then add 2 garlic cloves, minced. Add 500 grams ground beef and brown for 5 minutes. Stir in 400 grams crushed tomatoes and 2 tablespoons tomato paste, season with 1 teaspoons salt and 0.5 teaspoons black pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  2. Make the béchamel: Melt 40 grams butter in a saucepan over medium heat. Whisk in 40 grams all-purpose flour and cook for 5 minutes . Gradually pour in 500 milliliters whole milk, whisking constantly until smooth and thick. Season with 1 teaspoons salt and 0.3 teaspoons nutmeg.
  3. Assemble the lasagna: Preheat oven to 180°C. In a baking dish, layer meat sauce, 12 lasagna sheets, and béchamel. Repeat layers. Finish with béchamel on top and sprinkle with 80 grams grated parmesan and 150 grams grated mozzarella.
  4. Bake: Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes . Remove foil and bake for another 15 minutes until golden and bubbling.
  5. Rest and serve: Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
